Mozilla is…

a global community of people who believe that openness, innovation, and opportunity are key to the continued health of the Internet.

Mozilla is not just Firefox

And more at http://www.mozilla.org/en-US/products ....

Smart people are everywhere

  • 13 global offices and people working in more than 30 countries
  • Thousands of contributors (paid staff and volunteers)
  • Office space, Home Space, Coworking Space

Remotie - a person who works in a different geographic location than his/her manager.

Happy people are more productive

  • Flexible working hours
  • Contribute your time to what you're really care about
  • Your work is visible, everything is open sourced
  • Travelling is fun
  • Work from anywhere, nobody cares

Communication channels

  • Video meetings (Air Mozilla, Team wide, Company wide)
  • Work weeks, events, festivals, meetups and etc...
  • Weekly 1:1s
  • IRC - async and public
  • Email - async and public (mailing lists mostly)
  • Bugzilla (needinfo, review, feedback) - async and public

What about processes?

  • Module - is the discrete unit of code or activity
  • Module owners and Peers
  • Code review (peer review, super review, security review)
  • Bugzilla, GitHub, IRC discussions
  • Commit Access Levels
